Responsive Web Design, Development & Info Graphics

The Newfoundland Aquaculture Industry Association (NAIA) is a member-based non-profit organization that represents the interests of aquaculturists in Newfoundland and Labrador. The challenge was to promote their vision "To promote excellence in the aquaculture industry of Newfoundland and Labrador" with infographics incorporated in a responsive website. Using my photoshop and Illustrator skills to create engaging informative graphics for the user to expand across the whole screen size on any device.
