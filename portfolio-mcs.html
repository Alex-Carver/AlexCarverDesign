Media City Sun (MCS)

Media City Sun is a Tanning Salon in Burbank, California with the slogan "The best tanning salon in the valley".

The scope of work:

Home page design / Development

The Challenge:

To incorporate their logo colors into a responsive Homepage with eye-catching hover animations.

Tools:

Adobe XD, Photoshop, Illustrator, Html5 / CSS3, Bootstrap, and JS

The Solution:

In Collaboration with Big Sky Internet Design, I achieved this by using bright bold colors and gradients that matched the logo with their brand colors for the animation overlays. Focusing on what they had to offer and providing a professional but fun look.
