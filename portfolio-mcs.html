Media City Sun

Responsive Web Design & Development

Media City Sun is a Tanning Salon in Burbank, California with the slogan "The best tanning salon in the valley" The challenge was to incorporate their logo colors into a responsive Homepage with eye-catching hover animations. In Collaboration with Big Sky Internet Design, I achieved this by using bright bold colors and gradients that matched the logo with their brand colors for the animation overlays. Focusing on what they had to offer and providing a professional but fun look.
