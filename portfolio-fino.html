Fino Tequila

Responsive Web Design & Development

Fino is a Tequila company that's 100% women-owned based out of Chicago that just launched their product 4 months ago. The challenge was to create a whole new home page to showcase their products with a minimalistic clean style. Fino means "smooth" or "refined" with their slogan being "Failure is not an option". I achieved this by using smooth hover effects and a calculated bootstrap layout that is precise down to the pixel. Each picture focuses on a part of their brand making it easy for the user to navigate and learn about this amazing tequila.
