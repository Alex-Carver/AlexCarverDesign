Fino Tequila

Fino is a Tequila company that's 100% women-owned based out of Chicago that just launched their product. Fino means "smooth" or "refined" with their slogan being "Failure is not an option".

The scope of work:

Home page design / Development

The Challenge:

To create a whole new home page to showcase their products with a minimalistic clean style that represents their style of smooth and refined.

Tools:

Adobe XD, Photoshop, Illustrator, Html5 / CSS3, Bootstrap, and JS

The Solution:

I achieved this by using smooth hover effects and a calculated bootstrap layout that is precise down to the pixel. Each picture focuses on a part of their brand making it easy for the user to navigate and learn about this amazing tequila.
