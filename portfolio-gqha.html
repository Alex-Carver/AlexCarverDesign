Georgia Quarter Horse Association

Responsive Web Design & Development

The Georgia Quarter Horse Association is an affiliate of AQHA. The goal was an updated modern homepage design with a way to show all of their sponsors in a clean orderly fashion. Working in collaboration with Big Sky Internet Design, I achieved this by incorporating bold bright red colors to emphasize the brand, a sleek looking layout, and a clean responsive design that shows off the website on all screen sizes.
