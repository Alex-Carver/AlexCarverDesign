AMIR

Responsive Web Design, Logo, and Business Cards

Advanced Midwest Radiology (AMR) is a professional service corporation dedicated to providing a full spectrum of consultative diagnostic and interventional radiology services. The challenge was to create a whole new brand identity including a responsive website, logo, and business cards. I achieved this by first creating a logo that represented their specialty in "radiology" by including an MRI machine into the logo. Using those colors I then moved onto the business cards. I incorporated the logo as the focal point of the card with the doctor's names in bold to make it easier for the patient to read. From there, I moved onto the website. Using my photoshop skills I created an eye-catching background image with a clean style that flashes professionalism and expertise.
