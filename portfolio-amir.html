AMR

Responsive Web Design, Logo, and Business Cards

Advanced Midwest Radiology (AMR)

Is a professional service corporation dedicated to providing a full spectrum of consultative diagnostic and interventional radiology services.

The scope of work:

Complete website re-design / development, logo, business cards, and stickers.

The Challenge:

Creating a minimalistic clean look that boasts professionalism along with maintaining their new brand identity.

Tools:

Adobe XD, Photoshop, Illustrator, Html5 / CSS3, Bootstrap, and JS

The Solution:

Creating a new logo that incorporated their old brand guidelines was the first objective. Using the new logo, I established a whole new brand identity with the look and feel of a professional medical practice. I continued that look and feel across to the website and business cards.
