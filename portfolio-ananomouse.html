Ananomouse Solutions and Research

Is a company that focuses on finding matches for pedigrees with the ease of it's ai.

The scope of work:

Website Design / Development and Custom Graphics

The Challenge:

To present their new AI tool "Blood Hound 2.0" and what it does in an easy to digest manner for the user. The focal point was to showcase the app as an on the go tool no matter where the user is with their phone.

Tools:

Adobe XD, Photoshop, Illustrator, Html5 / CSS3, Bootstrap, and JS

The Solution:

Focusing on the goal at hand I came up with an easy to navigate website, engaging animations, and images to match the copy and not overwhelm the user with information while focusing on the mobile-friendly aspect of the application.
