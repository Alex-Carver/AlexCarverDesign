Ananomouse

Responsive Web Design & Development

Ananomouse Solutions and Research is a company that focuses on finding matches for pedigrees with the ease of it's ai. The challenge was to present their new AI tool "Blood Hound 2.0" and what it does in an easy to digest manner for the user as well as all whole new responsive design. It was a bit of a challenge as it's a very complicated and robust tool that is capable of rendering millions of possibilities to match pedigrees with ease. The focal point was to showcase it as an on the go tool no matter where the user is with their phone. I broke the website down into sections that explained everything to the user without overwhelming them with information. I achieved this with visually appealing pictures and devices that matched the copy.
