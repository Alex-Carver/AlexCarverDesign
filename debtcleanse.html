DebtCleanse

Your first step to financial freedom!

DebtCleanse™ was founded by Jorge P. Newbery, a successful entrepreneur who lost everything to a freak act of nature and found himself $26 million in debt. Jorge fought back and is now debt free. The DebtCleanse™ system was built around the strategies Jorge learned and developed in the process of eliminating his own debt.

Overview

As of the second quarter of 2020, about 1 in 2,756 properties are in foreclosure with about 376,052 housing foreclosures in the US. With the COVID-19 pandemic showing no signs of being over anytime soon, it is likely that there will be a substantial increase in foreclosures, short sales and bank-owned properties.

Problems

  • Outdated logo, website and app.
  • Reaching the target consumer.
  • Creating a bridge between the Attorney and consumer

Proposed Solutions

  • Creating a whole new brand identity.
  • User research and surveys to find out what the user is looking for.
  • Site mapping and A/B testing to follow the user's actions.

By creating a whole new brand identity while focusing on an intuitive interface for the website and consumer facing app this could help bring trust and a bigger user base.

Redesiging the Brand

The old logo was very outdated and wasn't very impactful.
Using trustworthy colors, a modern typeface, and a recognizable logo mark, DebtCleanse was reborn.

The Debtcleanse App

Understanding the user base was key to the success of this project. From ages 20-60 we had to make sure it was intuitve with a well executed user experience. Implementing the new color scheme, typography, and components helped the user identify exactly what they needed to do.
